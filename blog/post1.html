Blog | The Journey Begins

Welcome to the first post on my personal portfolio blog! This project marks the beginning of a journey to create a space that not only showcases my skills and experience but also serves as a living document of my growth as a developer.

The foundation of this site is built on simple, robust technologies: HTML5, CSS3, and a bit of JavaScript. I chose these to start with because they are the fundamental building blocks of the web, and a strong foundation is crucial for any project. The goal was to create a clean, responsive, and accessible user interface from scratch.

In the coming posts, I'll dive deeper into the specifics of the implementation, including the challenges I faced and the solutions I came up with. Stay tuned!
