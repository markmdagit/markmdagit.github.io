The Pivot: From Infrastructure to Engineering

With over eight years of experience at the heart of Amazon’s fulfillment infrastructure, I have built a career on solving complex technical problems under pressure. From managing site build-outs to deploying thousands of end-user devices, I’ve mastered the physical and networking layers that keep a global leader running.

Currently, I am leveraging that "boots-on-the-ground" technical foundation to transition into Computer Engineering. As a student at CSUSB, I am moving up the stack—combining my deep understanding of hardware and infrastructure with rigorous training in Assembly x86, C++, and API architecture.

My goal is to build software that is as reliable as the systems I’ve spent the last decade supporting. I don’t just write code; I understand the physical machines and networks it lives on.
