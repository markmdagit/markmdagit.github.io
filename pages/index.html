Cable Management

In the fast-paced environment of an Amazon warehouse, organized and efficient cable management is crucial. I led projects to overhaul the network racks, transforming them from a chaotic web of cables into a model of clarity and order. End User Equipment Deployments

I was responsible for the deployment of thousands of pieces of end-user equipment, from handheld scanners to laptops and desktops.
